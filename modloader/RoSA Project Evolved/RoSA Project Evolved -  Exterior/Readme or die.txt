------- Instructions:

Extract the folder "RoSA Project Evolved" to your ModLoader folder.

-- Download of Essentials Pack: https://www.mixmods.com.br/2019/06/sa-essentials-pack/

-- REQUIRES to increase the streaming memory limit, otherwise the objects will not load. Use at least one of them:

Open Limit Adjuster:
https://www.mixmods.com.br/2015/02/open-limit-adjuster/

MixSets (StreamMemory at least 512, recommended 1024):
https://www.mixmods.com.br/2021/08/sa-mixsets/



 == Compatibility:
 ModLoader considers .img files to always have priority below all other files.
 So, avoid installing original texture mods in modloader as they will remove HD textures.
 
 This mod already includes SA Optimized Map (optimized map textures). Remove it if you use it.

 If you use Proper Fixes, LOWER the priority of RoSA in ModLoader, or increase from Proper Fixes:
 https://www.mixmods.com.br/2015/07/tutorial-dicas-tudo-sobre-modloader-gta/#Priority

 For SAMP, download its own version, otherwise it will be without some textures:
 https://www.mixmods.com.br/2021/08/rosa-project-evolved-remaster-texturas-hd/
